Hazard Symbols  
Hazard Symbols Many countries require Hazard Symbols labeled on the containers of chemicals . These symbols have the following meanings:

　E: Explosive
　

　T: Toxic
　

　O: Oxidizing agent
　

　Xn: Harmful
　

　F++: Extremely flammable
　

　Xi: Irritant
　

　F+: Highly flammable
　

　C: Corrosive
　

　F: Flammable
　

　N: Dangerous for the environment
　

　T+: Very toxic
